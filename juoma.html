May the juomapeli begin!

This is a simple drinking game. Juoma means "a drink" in Finnish.

Rules are simple

  • List players
  • List available drinks
  • The person who rolls the highest number has to drink the drink provided by the game, player can decide portion size
  • If you puke or pass out, you lose
  • If a drink runs out, it must be removed from the drink list
  • Last one who's not thrown up or passed out, IS THE WINNER!
  • Cocktail Hour: If the highest rolling player doesn't drink the cocktail, player has lost the game
  • Do not die
  • Have fun
